### 1. Project Overview

**Vision:** To provide the intelligent infrastructure for every Indian educator, making high-quality, personalized education accessible and scalable through AI.

**Purpose:** Learnivo Ai is a sophisticated AI-powered teaching assistant designed to automate the administrative heavy-lifting of teaching. By leveraging deep domain-specific AI flows, Learnivo handles lesson planning, multimodal content generation, and intelligent assessments, allowing teachers to focus on student mentorship and pedagogical excellence.

### 4. Market Differentiation

1.  **Curriculum Awareness:** Unlike ChatGPT, Learnivo is aware of CBSE, ICSE, and State Board frameworks.
2.  **Cultural Intelligence:** Learnivo contextualizes physics using "Mumbai Local Trains" rather than "London Undergrounds".
3.  **Privacy-First:** Data is logically isolated via multi-tenancy on Firebase, ensuring school data doesn't leak into general LLM training pools.
