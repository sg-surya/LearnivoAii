# Sahayak AI - School Admin Workflow & Feature List

Jab ek school humara platform subscribe karta hai, to Admin ka kaam in steps mein aage badhta hai:

---

### Step 1: School ki Buniyad Rakhna (Initial Setup) 
*Yeh first-time setup hai jo poore school ke liye foundation taiyar karta hai.*

- **School Profile Complete Karna:** School ka naam, address, logo, contact details etc. daalna.
- **Academic Session Banana:** Jaise "2024-2025" session ko active karna.
- **Master Data Define Karna:** School mein padhaye jaane wale sabhi Subjects aur Classes ki ek list banana.

---

### Step 2: Logon ko Jodna (User Onboarding)
*School ka structure ready hone ke baad, ab logon ko add karne ka time hai.*

- **Teachers ko Add Karna:** Teachers ki profile banana, unhe login ID/password dena, aur unhe subjects assign karna.
- **Students ko Add Karna:** Students ka admission karna, unhe unki class aur section mein daalna, aur unke parents ko link karna.
- **Parents ko Add Karna:** Students ki profile ke saath unke parents ke accounts jodna.

---

### Step 3: School Chalana (Academic Operations)
*Ab school ko day-to-day chalane ka time hai.*

- **Timetable Banana:** Har class aur teacher ke liye weekly timetable design karna.
- **Syllabus Plan Karna:** Har subject ke liye syllabus define karna.

---

### Step 4: Rozana ka Management aur Nigrani (Day-to-day Management & Monitoring)
*School ab chal raha hai, aur Admin ka kaam monitoring aur communication ka hai.*

- **Announcements Karna:** School-wide ya class-specific notice bhejna.
- **Analytics Dekhna:** Attendance, academic performance, aur platform usage par nazar rakhna.
- **Pareshani Suljhana:** Password reset karna, roles change karna, etc.

---

## School Admin Dashboard - 30 Essential Features

Is flow ke hisab se, ek School Admin ke dashboard mein yeh 30 features hone chahiye:

1.  **School Profile Editor:** School ka naam, logo, address update karne ke liye.
2.  **Academic Session Manager:** Naye sessions (like 2025-2026) banane, archive karne aur activate karne ke liye.
3.  **Class & Section Manager:** Nayi classes (Grade 1, Grade 2) aur unke sections (A, B, C) banane ke liye.
4.  **Subject Manager:** School mein padhaye jaane wale sabhi subjects ki master list banane aur edit karne ke liye.
5.  **School-wide Settings:** Default language, time zone, aur grading system set karne ke liye.
6.  **Subscription & Billing:** Apna current plan dekhne, bills pay karne aur usage track karne ke liye ek portal.
7.  **Branding Customization:** Platform ko school ke colors aur logo ke saath customize karne ka option.
8.  **Bulk User Upload:** Excel/CSV file se ek saath saikdon (hundreds of) teachers ya students ko add karne ki facility.
9.  **Individual User Profile:** Kisi bhi user (teacher, student) ki profile dekhne aur edit karne ki facility.
10. **Role Assignment:** Users ko roles (teacher, student, parent, admin) assign karna.
11. **Password Reset:** Kisi bhi user ke liye password reset karne ki ability.
12. **Deactivate/Activate User:** Kisi account ko temporarily suspend karne ke liye.
13. **Parent-Student Linking:** Ek student ke saath ek ya zyada parents ko jodne ke liye ek interface.
14. **AI Timetable Generator:** Classes, teachers aur subjects ke basis par automatically ek clash-free timetable banane wala tool.
15. **Substitute Teacher Assignment:** Agar koi teacher absent hai to jaldi se ek substitute teacher assign karne ke liye.
16. **Announcement Dashboard:** Poore school, specific grades, ya sirf teachers ko notice bhejne ke liye.
17. **Event Calendar:** School ki holidays, exams, aur events ko mark karne ke liye ek school-wide calendar.
18. **Circular Generator:** PDF format mein official circulars banane aur distribute karne ke liye.
19. **Fee Management (Future mein):** Fee structures define karne aur payments track karne ke liye ek module.
20. **Transport Management (Future mein):** Bus routes aur student transport details manage karne ke liye.
21. **Library Module:** School library ki books ko list aur track karne ke liye.
22. **Syllabus Tracker:** Yeh dekhne ke liye ki har class mein syllabus ka kitna percent complete ho gaya hai.
23. **Main Dashboard:** Total students, teachers, daily attendance percentage, aur recent activities ka ek bird's-eye view.
24. **Attendance Reports:** Poore school, kisi specific class, ya ek single student ke liye attendance reports generate karna.
25. **Academic Performance Reports:** Yeh dekhne ke liye ki kaun si classes ya subjects accha perform kar rahe hain aur kahan improvement ki zaroorat hai.
26. **Platform Usage Reports:** Yeh dekhne ke liye ki kaun se teachers aur students AI tools ka sabse zyada istemal kar rahe hain.
27. **Student Report Card Generator:** Sabhi subjects ke marks ko milakar official report cards banane ke liye.
28. **Logs & Audit Trails:** Yeh track karne ke liye ki kis admin ne system mein kya changes kiye hain.
29. **Resource Usage:** Yeh dekhne ke liye ki kaun sa study material (jaise AI-generated worksheets) sabse zyada download kiya ja raha hai.
30. **Feedback Collection:** Teachers aur students se platform ke baare mein feedback aur suggestions collect karne ke liye ek central jagah.

Yeh sabhi features milakar, School Admin ko apne institution ko effectively manage karne ke liye ek complete aur powerful control center de denge.
